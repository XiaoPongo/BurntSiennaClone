Introduction
I am a passionate young artist who paints on canvas and clothes. I am also the founder and host of 'La Velata'.
La Velata is an interactive, entertaining and insightful fabric painting workshop, held on Sundays at 'The Southern Deck'. La Velata aims to provide the guests and attendees with the opportunity to paint with their friends & families, meet new people, create bonds and share ideas while enjoying the beautiful view of the beach and sunset, while enjoying the top notch food and hospitality of 'The Southern Deck - Beach Bar and Bistro'
The idea behind La Velata
The idea behind La Velata is not to stress you about creating better art or to complete with one and other but to explore and identify your own self. By providing you with basic information and basic art materials, La Velata will guide and assist you in recreating and painting a reflection of your emotions, ideas or beliefs on a material that stays close to you, so that you can wear or display it proudly.

What I do
Whether you are looking for a traditional portrait painting or cool customized drip, I will bring your vision to life.
Canvas painting

I craft canvas paintings deeply inspired by emotions, back story and philosophy. Using bold colors and intricate details, I aim to evoke personal interpretations, making each piece an unique experience for the viewers.

Fabric Painting

I transfer your favorite hoodie, jacket or baggy jeans into one – of a kind art. I hand paint intricate designs that resonate with your personality and style, creating functional art you can wear and display.

La Velata

I plan and host a very entertaining, interactive and insightful art workshops, guiding the attendees to paint on fabric or any other material, along with their friends and families creating beautiful memories that they can lock into their chest for lifetime.
